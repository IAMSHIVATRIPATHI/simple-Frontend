Welcome To Our Cafe Emerald

We are delighted to have you here! Enjoy the finest coffee and pastries at Cafe Emerald.

Espresso

A strong and bold coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Latte

A creamy coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.

Cappuccino

A balanced coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

Americano

A smooth coffee drink made by diluting espresso with hot water.

Mocha

A rich coffee drink made with espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup.
